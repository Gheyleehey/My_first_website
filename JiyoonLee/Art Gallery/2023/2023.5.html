On an act of sensing, Single channel video installation (2mins 24sec), HD Monitor, paper, PVC electrical insulation tape, 2023

On an act of Sensing: Space (2023)

This project is a process of art practice exploring muscle/sensorial memory, drawing upon her international resident who experienced a different metric system. In the process, Jiyoon uses her somatic experience, the body and its rhythms, as the main material to understand the various systems of her new environments.
