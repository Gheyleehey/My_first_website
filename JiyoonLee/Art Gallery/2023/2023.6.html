2023 | Loud Steps

Loud steps, Performance art, PVC Sheet, Duration 2-3mins, 2023

‘Loud steps’ is a minimalism-style performance focused on the movement of the feet on a PVC sheet and minor sounds generated by physical interaction between the body and the environment. By creating an immersive experience that amplified different sensory organs—and breaking this experience in the ending scene with a loud inhaling breath—the performance attempted to create tension within a sense of space.

It is, rather, a space reckoned starting from me as the null point or degree zero of spatiality. I do not see it according to its exterior envelope; I live it from the inside; I am immersed in it.— Maurice Merleau-Ponty.
